# BKK Pattaya Private Taxi

## Product Purpose
High-conversion SEO landing page and booking flow for a private taxi transfer service between Bangkok and Pattaya, Thailand. The site IS the product — it must convert international tourists into booking customers.

## Users
- **Primary**: International tourists (English-speaking) arriving at Suvarnabhumi/Don Mueang airports
- **Secondary**: Expats living in Bangkok/Pattaya corridor needing regular transfers
- **Tertiary**: Business travelers seeking reliable, private transport
- **Context**: Users are typically on mobile, possibly jet-lagged, in an unfamiliar country, and need to feel safe booking with an unknown service

## Brand
- **Tone**: Premium, trustworthy, professional yet approachable. "Your trusted friend who happens to run the best taxi service"
- **Register**: brand (landing page — design IS the product)
- **Visual Identity**: Midnight Blue & Champagne Gold — conveys luxury, trust, and professionalism without being unapproachable
- **Anti-references**: Generic taxi apps (Grab-style), cheap-looking transport comparison sites, over-designed travel agencies

## Strategic Principles
1. **Trust first** — visitors must feel safe booking instantly with a service they've never heard of
2. **Price transparency** — fixed pricing eliminates the #1 anxiety ("will I get scammed?")
3. **Premium positioning** — justify higher price vs public transport/taxis through quality signals
4. **Frictionless booking** — WhatsApp/LINE as familiar, low-commitment booking channels
5. **Social proof** — reviews, ratings, and trust badges reduce perceived risk

## Tech Stack
- React 19 + TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS 4 (OKLCH color space)
- Framer Motion (animations)
- Radix UI + shadcn/ui (accessible components)
- i18n: English + Thai
